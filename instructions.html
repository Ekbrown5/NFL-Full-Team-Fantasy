How To Play

Full team fantasy is played very differently than the normal fantasy league.

Instead of choosing individual players, you're choosing an NFL team as a whole.

Everybody will have different teams. You can't select the same team twic.

The objective is to have the most points in the league at the end of the season.

Draft

Each user will take turns drafting their team. There will be 3 rounds in which you will select 3 different teams.

Once somebody selects a team, that team will be taken off the board.

Points

Every win that your team gets is a point that you will recieve. The more points the better.

If all 3 of your teams win in the same week, you will recieve a double point bonus.

NFL teams get a one week break during the season called a bye week.

If one of your teams are on a bye week, you will not be eligable to recieve a double point bonus.
